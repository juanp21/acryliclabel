Acrylic Label is an experiential record and label services company based in Los Angeles, California, staying true to our musical ethos through a strong curative message.

We assemble a global, multicultural melting pot of artists around conceptual projects. Music as a tether: focus, rhythm, and relief from our bustling days.

We are our artists and our artists are us.
